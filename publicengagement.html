    Training

    I deliver CPD sessions on sci comm as part of UWE's science communication masterclass and UWE's "Science Communication Building Blocks".

    I run public speaking courses to increase diversity in participation in meetings within the Labour Party and trade unions. Courses focuss on confidence and structure.

    I judge public speaking competitions for science communicators, most recently the "3 minute wonder" competition for the Institute of Physics in the West of England.
